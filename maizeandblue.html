Maize and Blue Cupboard

Our mission

To ensure members of the University of Michigan community— whether on a tight budget or physically restrained from getting to a grocery store—receive equitable access to healthy, nutritious, and nourishing food and the ability to prepare it for themselves or others.

How can we help?

Maize and Blue Cupboard is more than just a resource for food assistance; it's a community dedicated to supporting the well-being of every University of Michigan student. We understand that access to nutritious food is a fundamental part of academic success and personal growth. By choosing Maize and Blue Cupboard, you are choosing a compassionate support system that empowers you with the resources, education, and community connections you need to thrive. Let us help you focus on your studies without the worry of food insecurity.

How Can We Help?

Maize and Blue Cupboard is dedicated to providing essential services and resources to ensure that every member of our community has access to healthy food and the knowledge needed to prepare nourishing meals. Here’s how we can support you:

  • Food Assistance: Access a variety of fresh produce, pantry staples, and other grocery items to help you maintain a balanced diet, regardless of your financial situation.
  • Nutrition Education: Join our workshops on nutrition and meal planning to learn how to make the most of the foods you have access to. We provide tips and recipes that promote healthy, affordable eating habits.
  • Cooking Classes: Participate in hands-on cooking classes where you’ll learn basic culinary skills and how to prepare nutritious meals. Our classes are designed to be practical and accessible for all skill levels.
  • Supportive Community: Connect with other students and members of the university community who share similar experiences. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels supported and respected.
  • Resource Referrals: Our team can connect you with additional resources on campus and beyond, including financial assistance, wellness programs, and other support services that contribute to your overall well-being.
