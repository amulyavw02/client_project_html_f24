Center for the Education of Women +

Our mission

At CEW+, we navigate circumstantial barriers by providing academic, financial, and professional support to help you reach your personal potential. Established to support women through higher education, we lift up women and all underserved communities at U-M and beyond. Through career and education counseling, funding, workshops, events, and a diverse, welcoming community, we exist to empower. We are CEW+, and we’re here to help you reach your potential.

Why Choose CEW+?

At CEW+, we believe in the power of education and personal growth for everyone. We’re committed to supporting not only your academic journey but also your personal and professional development. Our resources and services are designed with you in mind, whether you're overcoming barriers, advancing your career, or seeking a supportive community. Join us and become part of a network that champions inclusivity, resilience, and lifelong learning.

How CEW+ Can Help You

CEW+ offers a comprehensive range of services tailored to support the unique needs of our community members. Whether you're seeking career guidance, financial assistance, or personal development resources, we're here to empower you. Discover how our programs can help you overcome challenges, build connections, and achieve your goals.

  • Career Counseling: Our career counselors provide personalized guidance to help you explore career options, develop skills, and create a strategic plan for your future. We offer resume reviews, interview preparation, and job search strategies tailored to your goals.
  • Scholarships and Funding: CEW+ offers a variety of scholarships, grants, and emergency funding to support your education and well-being. These resources are available for undergraduate, graduate, and non-traditional students facing financial challenges.
  • Workshops and Events: Join our workshops and events that cover topics such as financial literacy, stress management, leadership, and skill-building. Connect with peers and professionals to expand your network and knowledge.
  • Mental Health Resources: We offer access to mental health resources, including counseling and wellness programs, designed to support your emotional well-being. Our services aim to help you manage stress, improve focus, and maintain a healthy balance.
  • Advocacy and Community Support: Our advocacy programs provide a platform for underserved communities, helping you find your voice and advocate for issues that matter to you. Engage in community discussions, campaigns, and volunteer opportunities.
